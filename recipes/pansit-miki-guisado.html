Pansit Miki Guisado

For when there's a camera-on team video call at 1 p.m.

Quick, spicy, and filling!

(Remember to defrost the shrimps at least half an hour before cooking.)

Prep: 15 mins    Cook: 10 mins    Servings: 2


Ingredients

  • 250 g miki noodles
  • 10-12 pieces of shrimp
  • 1 small red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 head of bok choy
  • Mixed veggies
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • 1/4 chicken cube
  • Water
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat up water in the pan. When it boils, put in the chicken cube. Let it dissolve, then put in your noodles and let them boil for 1 minute. Drain the noodles, reserving a little bit of the chicken broth. Set aside.
  2. Chop up your onion, garlic, and bok choy. In a bowl, make your noodle sauce by combining soy sauce, oyster sauce, chicken broth, and sesame oil.
  3. Sauté the garlic and onion in olive oil, followed by the shrimps and then the mixed veggies. Pour in the noodle sauce, followed by the noodles. Toss for a bit before finally adding the bok choy. Lil more time in the pan then it's good to go! Totally acceptable to eat straight from the pan.
